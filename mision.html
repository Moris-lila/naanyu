MISSION

Naanyu CBO is committed to promote social, educational, economic and cultural development of women and girls. .

Sustainable Livelihoods Through Community Empowerment

We do this by advocating for women’s rights, advancing women’s capacities and entrepreneurship skills, promoting their participation in the decisions that affect their lives, and amplifying their voices for climate change action and wildlife conservation.

Empowerment

When women are empowered, the pool of people committed to resource conservation is enlarged, children are raised with a conservation ethic, and a sustainable green economy can be fully realized.
