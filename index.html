About us

Established in 2023, Naanyu Community Development Network (NCDN) works to empower women and girls through the promotion of cultural entrepreneurship in Kajiado County.

Stimulating Rural Community

Our programs focus on stimulating rural community rights among the pastoralist communities, empowering women as key changemakers in environment and wildlife conservation, preservation of Maasai’s heritage, cultural diversity, and living arts/beadwork while promoting their social and economic potential. Our approach embodies the maximum possible contribution from each of the three pillars of environmental, economic and social sustainability.

Reach Out

Beading for lasting change:

The story behind the beading women presents a great opportunity advocating for women’s rights, eliminating discrimination against women including GBV, advancing women’s capacities and leadership skills, promoting their participation in the decisions that affect their lives, and amplifying their voices for wildlife conservation and peace. Although still marginalized and burdened by multiple work, they are proving that they can actively engage in their community’s economic development rather than remain passive receivers of programs and policy.
